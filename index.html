About

Hi, I’m Brenda and I am interested in building meaningful applications or games for society.

I enjoy the process of design as we would never know where we would get to but we would go through iterations of iterations before finally being relatively satisfied with a design. However, design does not stop there. There is always room for improvement and the best way to improve is to listen to the users’ feedback.

I aspire to create games that can make an impact on our lives; Games with meaningful stories and characters that may be able to inculcate certain values in the players or increase their awareness about a certain issue; Games which allow us to connect with our family, friends and even to make new friends around the world.

I hope that games can not only allow its players to relax and bring joy to them, but also allow players to learn something new :)

Episode Interactive

Episode is an app which provides a platform for users to write and direct their stories by coding. When I was just a reader, I wondered how these interactive stories were written, so I decided to try it out. I enjoyed wrting and coding my story and even participating in Episode's writing contest. Although I did not win for that contest, I was selected to be featured on the "Asian Voices" shelf for a week.

Mr Love: Producers' Secrets

I met some friends living in the South East Asian region through Facebook, by a common interest - a game known as "Mr Love: Dream Date". It is a romance simulation game and, unexpectedly, I enjoyed the main storyline and how the characters grow as the story progresses.

One day, my friends and I decided to create a fanpage for this game to post memes, which we had made while chatting. I took the initiative to design our fanpage profile picture, logo, and cover photo, as shown in the picture above. Our fanpage eventually moved to posting videos rather than just images. Currently, I am the the page admin who is in charge of creating content and managing the posts.

I enjoy being part of this group which we call MLPS (Mr Love: Producers' Secrets).

Mush!

This is a school project to create a text-based game using Python programming language. It is a role-playing game where the player explores the room and encounters a mushroom monster which the player has to fight. This was my first time coding a game and I enjoyed the process as I was able to to express my creativity while practising my coding skills. You may watch a video of a demo of the game on the left button or read a documentation of how the game was planned.

Courage . Purpose . Character

This is a stop motion video made in 2017, for a Chinese Language event in school. The given theme was Meridian Junior Colllege's school values - Courage, Purpose and Character. I came up with the idea of 3 characters being stranded on an island and they have to work together in order to return home. These 3 characters each represent the 3 school values. I did not know there was a contest then but was notified that the video had won a prize. You may watch the video by tapping on the button below.

P.S. The video is in Chinese and its speed is fast due to the 1.5-minute time limit.

Hourglass Android App

This is a school group project of developing an Android app. Our app aims to motivate its users to limit their phone usage, in order to better manage their time and have sufficient rest. The pictures displayed here are some of the iterations of user interface we have come up with. We used Android Studio to build the app. I was in charge of the front end design of the app.

CoFe Toilet

This is a school design project about improving the layout toilets for the pandemic. The problem we focused to solve was social distancing in toilets. We researched and studied the toilet designs in our surroundings in order to understand how toilets are designed.

Although it was tedious to create numerous iterations of our toilet layout design, I enjoyed the design process. The pros and cons for each layout change with each iteration and we have to "experiment" to obtain the best solution. You may watch a video which summarises our project or visit the website to find out more.

P.S. Project Part 4 in the website summarises our final product.
